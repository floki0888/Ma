Madelyn Beaufils is a Sydney‑based vocalist and guitarist with a captivating stage presence and a versatile repertoire spanning pop, rock, jazz, blues and Brazilian music. Singing in both English and Portuguese, she delivers unforgettable performances for weddings, corporate functions, festivals and private celebrations.

About

Madelyn Beaufils is a polished, soulful performer known for her rich voice and international flair. Whether performing solo acoustic, with a duo, or as part of a full band, she brings energy and elegance to every event.

Fluent in English and Portuguese, Madelyn connects deeply with audiences and creates moments to remember. Her professionalism, versatility and passion make her the ideal choice for weddings, corporate functions and celebrations of all kinds.
